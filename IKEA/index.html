What is IKEA?
  • IKEA is one of the biggest furniture companies in the world, with over 400 stores worldwide, selling affordable household accessories to its customers.
  • IKEA is a multinational group of companies - founded in Sweden - that sells ready-to-assemble furniture, kitchen ware and home accessories.
  • IKEA is the biggest furniture company in the world, with over 400 stores worldwide, selling affordable household items to millions of customers. It was started by Ingvar Kamprad in 1943, who was 17-year-old at the time, in Sweden.

    • Core Competencies

  • Diverse functionality - Family-club for savings and free coffee/tea every time you visit,complimentary childcare services.
  • Good Quality- The products are designed in Switzerland and manufactued in developing countries to ensure quality at a userfriendly price.
  • Low Costs- The company provides furniture to its customers that is 20-40% lower cost than that of its competitors with no extra charges included.
  • Unique Design Capabilities- Rather than being sold pre-assembled, much of IKEA's furniture is designed to be assembled by the customer.
